Team

Prof. Chris Evans

Director – Head of Science

Chris is an expert in the measurement and mitigation of greenhouse gas emissions from drained peatlands, ranging from the English lowlands to the Welsh uplands, as well as Southeast Asia and South America. He helped to develop global standard protocols for peatland emissions reporting as a Lead Author of the IPCC Wetlands Supplement, and led the implementation of this methodology in the UK’s National Atmospheric Emissions Inventory. He currently leads major peatland research programmes for Defra, UK Research and Innovation and others, and provides regular advice to governments, NGOs and the private sector on climate change mitigation and adaptation.

Dr. Ben Roberts

Director – Chief Enterprise Officer

Specialist in instrumentation, remote sensing, and greenhouse gas removal projects, with a focus on peatlands. Executive Committee member of the UK GGR Future Leaders Network (2023-2025), and member of the Wales Peatland Evidence Group.
