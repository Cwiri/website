Privacy & Cookies

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Overview

Cwiri Ltd is the data controller for personal data collected through this website. We are committed to handling personal data responsibly and in accordance with UK GDPR.

We do not use this website for third-party advertising. We only collect personal data that you choose to provide, together with any limited technical or cookie data associated with the operation of embedded third-party services such as Brevo.

What data we collect

We may collect and process personal data that you voluntarily provide when you complete a contact form, use the website chat function, subscribe to updates, or contact us by email. This may include your name, email address, organisation, project details, and any other information you choose to provide in your message.

How we use your data

We use personal data to respond to enquiries, manage communications, assess potential business opportunities, provide information you have requested, and, where relevant, manage subscriptions or follow-up contact. The lawful basis for this processing is usually our legitimate interests in operating and developing our business, and in some cases taking steps at your request before entering into a contract. Where you choose to subscribe to updates or marketing communications, we may rely on your consent.

We use Brevo to provide website chat, embedded contact forms, and related communication tools. Where you use these features, the information you submit may be processed by Brevo on our behalf in accordance with our instructions. For more information, see Brevo's privacy policy.

Cookies

This website may use strictly necessary cookies or similar technologies in connection with embedded third-party services such as Brevo, including for functionality, service delivery, security, and user interaction. We do not use advertising cookies or behavioural tracking cookies.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you may have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information, request deletion of your personal data, request restriction of processing, object to processing in certain circumstances, and request transfer of your data where applicable.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact form on this website or by email at the address available on our Contact page. We may need to verify your identity before responding to your request.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). Our ICO Registration number is ZC052947.

Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, including responding to enquiries, maintaining appropriate business records, meeting legal or regulatory obligations, and resolving disputes. Where information is no longer required, we will delete it or anonymise it where appropriate.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to our website, services, or legal obligations. The date of the most recent revision will always be noted below.
