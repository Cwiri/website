Norfolk Broad Peatland Deployment 9 dipwells, sensors and LoRaWAN gateway

Infrastructure, sensors and telemetry delivered in one coordinated field campaign.

Cwiri undertook a recent deployment in the Norfolk Broad with the installation of nine 3 m SMART dipwells, integrated sensors, and a dedicated LoRaWAN gateway. The installation phase was completed smoothly and efficiently, with strong planning and preparation enabling confident delivery on site.

Project Scope

Install and commission a robust, real-time peatland water-level monitoring network.

Delivery Highlights

  • 9 x 3 m dipwells installed
  • Sensor systems integrated and commissioned
  • 1 x LoRaWAN gateway deployed on site

Outcome

Near-instant field data now streams to dashboard views, supporting restoration decisions and programme reporting.

"Lovely day installing our series of SMART dipwells with the brilliant team from Cwiri. The data will beam directly to a dashboard via Norfolk and Suffolk's largest free-to-use public sector long-range wide area network (LoRaWAN) in the UK. Our monitoring is funded by the Defra / Natural England Nature for Climate Peatland Grant and supports our ongoing peatland restoration work. Instant data = so cool!"

Andrea Kelly, Environment Policy Adviser, Broads Authority

Partners and stakeholders: Broads Authority, New Anglia Local Enterprise Partnership, Norfolk County Council, Suffolk County Council.
